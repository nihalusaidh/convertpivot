How to Convert FIT to CSV

Garmin FIT files contain detailed workout data including GPS coordinates, heart rate, cadence, speed, and power. Our converter extracts all GPS records and outputs them as a structured CSV with columns for every metric.

Use the CSV data in Excel to create heart rate zone charts, analyze pacing over distance, generate elevation profiles, or calculate training load metrics. The tabular format makes it easy to filter, sort, and visualize your performance data.

FIT files come from Garmin Forerunner, Fenix, Vivoactive, and Edge devices. You can export them from Garmin Connect or download directly from your device.
