Frequently Asked Questions

An anagram is a word or phrase formed by rearranging the letters of another word or phrase. For example, 'listen' and 'silent' are anagrams of each other. Anagrams are commonly used in word games, puzzles, and cryptography. The best anagrams use all the original letters exactly once.
