Why Convert ICS to CSV?

Calendar files (ICS) are great for importing events between apps, but they're hard to analyze, sort, or edit. Converting to CSV gives you spreadsheet power: sort by date, filter by location, calculate durations, and create reports. Use it for auditing your calendar, migrating between platforms, or analyzing your time allocation.

Our converter extracts: Event Title, Start Date, End Date, Start Time, End Time, Location, Description, Status, and UID for each event.

Frequently Asked Questions

ICS (iCalendar) is a standard file format for calendar data. It stores events, appointments, tasks, and free/busy time information. ICS files are used by Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Outlook, and most calendar apps to share events.
