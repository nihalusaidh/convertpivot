How to Convert GPX to CSV

GPX files from GPS devices, running watches, and smartphone tracking apps contain rich geographic data. Our parser extracts every point from waypoints, track segments, and routes — including latitude, longitude, elevation, timestamp, name, and description.

The CSV output includes a "type" column that identifies each point as a waypoint, trackpoint, or routepoint so you can filter and sort your data in Excel. Use the data for elevation profile charts, distance calculations, speed analysis, or import into any database.

This is particularly useful for athletes analyzing training routes, researchers working with GPS fieldwork data, and outdoor enthusiasts cataloging their adventures.
