HEIC vs JPEG vs WebP vs AVIF — Image Format Comparison

July 13, 2026

Image formats have come a long way. JPEG was king for decades, but newer formats like HEIC, WebP, and AVIF give you way better compression and features. Whether you're a photographer choosing a shooting format, a developer trying to speed up your site, or an iPhone user wondering why your photos are HEIC instead of JPEG, this image format comparison has you covered.

Each format targets a different use case. JPEG is the universal standard — everything supports it. HEIC is Apple's modern format for iPhone photos. WebP is Google's web-focused format. AVIF is the newest kid on the block with the best compression but the least support. Which one you pick depends on what you're trying to do.
