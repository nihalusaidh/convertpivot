How to Convert EPUB to PDF

July 13, 2026

EPUB is great for reading on phones and e-readers because text reflows to fit your screen. But sometimes you need something fixed — maybe you want to print a chapter, share a section with someone who does not have an e-reader, or send a PDF to a publisher.

Converting EPUB to PDF gives you a print-ready document with page numbers and fixed formatting. Fonts, margins, page breaks — everything stays exactly as you set it. Here is how to do it for free.

What is the difference between EPUB and PDF? ▼EPUB reflows text to fit your screen — great for phones and e-readers. PDF locks the layout — great for printing and sharing fixed-format documents.Can I convert EPUB to PDF for free? ▼Yes. Calibre is free and powerful. Online converters also work for occasional use. No payment needed.Will my formatting be preserved? ▼Mostly, but expect some differences. EPUB is fluid, PDF is fixed. Font sizes, margins, and line spacing may shift a bit.Can I convert DRM-protected EPUBs? ▼Not with standard tools. Removing DRM first may violate copyright. Only convert files you own that are DRM-free.What is the best EPUB to PDF converter? ▼Calibre for desktop — it handles batch conversion and gives you fine control. Online converters for quick one-off jobs. I use Calibre for my library and an online tool for single books.
