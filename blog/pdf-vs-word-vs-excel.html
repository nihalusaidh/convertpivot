PDF vs Word vs Excel — Document Format Comparison

July 13, 2026

Three document formats dominate the professional world: PDF, Word (DOCX), and Excel (XLSX). Each one serves a fundamentally different purpose, yet people mix them up or use them interchangeably all the time. Understanding when to reach for each format makes a huge difference in how effectively you communicate — whether it's for business, school, or personal projects.

PDF is the gold standard for sharing documents — it looks exactly the same on every device. Word is what you use to create and edit text documents with other people. Excel is the go-to for crunching numbers, organizing data, and building financial models. This document format comparison breaks down the strengths and best use cases for each one.
