Automated Column Mapping

The converter automatically recognizes these column headers: Full Name, First Name, Last Name, Email, Phone, Company, Job Title, Address, Website, Birthday, and Notes. Additional columns are ignored. For best results, ensure your CSV has a header row and uses standard column names.
