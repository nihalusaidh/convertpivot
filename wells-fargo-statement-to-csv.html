Built for Wells Fargo Statement Layouts

Wells Fargo statements have a distinct layout with transaction details listed in a table format. This converter is specifically tuned to parse Wells Fargo's statement structure, including the date column on the left, description in the center, and withdrawal/deposit amounts on the right. It correctly handles the running balance column that Wells Fargo includes on most statements. The parser processes multi-page statements sequentially and assembles all transactions into a single CSV output. Both personal and business Wells Fargo accounts are supported. Business statements often include additional columns like transaction codes — these are preserved in the CSV output. For the most reliable extraction, ensure you download the text-based PDF from Wells Fargo's website rather than using a scanned copy. Scanned PDFs lack the embedded text layer needed for extraction and may produce incomplete results.
