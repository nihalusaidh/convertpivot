How to Convert SVG to PNG

SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics) is the standard format for vector graphics on the web. Unlike pixel-based formats, SVG defines shapes mathematically, allowing infinite scaling without quality loss. However, many applications require raster formats like PNG.

Our converter renders your SVG onto an HTML Canvas at your specified resolution and exports as a transparent PNG. You can control the output dimensions — useful for generating specific icon sizes, social media images, or print-ready assets.

This is particularly valuable for designers who create vectors in tools like Figma, Illustrator, or Inkscape and need to generate PNG assets for app development, web use, or client delivery.
