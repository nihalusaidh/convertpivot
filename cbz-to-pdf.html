How to Use the CBZ to PDF Converter

Select a CBZ comic book archive file from your device. The tool extracts all page images from the ZIP archive, sorts them by filename, and generates a PDF document with each page image on its own page. This is ideal for reading comics on devices that don't support CBZ, sharing with others, or archiving your collection in a universal format.

Each page is scaled to fit the PDF page dimensions while preserving the original aspect ratio. Images are embedded at full resolution for the best quality output.
