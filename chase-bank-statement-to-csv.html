How to Convert Chase Bank Statement to CSV

1

Download from Chase

Log in to Chase online banking and download your statement as PDF. Go to Statements & Documents, select your account and date range, then click Download.

2

Drop into Converter

Drag the Chase PDF onto the drop zone above or click to browse and select the file. The converter will extract every transaction from the statement pages.

3

Download CSV

Click Extract & Convert to CSV. Your transactions are parsed and a CSV file downloads automatically. Open it in Excel or import into your accounting software.

Optimized for Chase Statement Format

Chase bank statements follow a consistent layout with transaction date, description, and amount columns on the right. The converter is specifically tuned to recognize this format, making it more reliable than generic PDF converters. It handles multi-page statements, multiple accounts within a single PDF, and correctly separates debit and credit amounts into their own CSV columns. Chase business account statements are also supported, though the column layout may differ slightly. For the most accurate results, download the PDF version of your statement rather than a printed or scanned copy. Text-based PDFs from Chase's online banking portal yield near-perfect conversion rates. If you have a large number of statements to process, simply repeat the conversion for each PDF — there are no limits or usage caps.
