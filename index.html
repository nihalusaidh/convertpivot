Why ConvertPivot?

Most online file converters work the same way: you upload your file to their server, they convert it, you download the result. The trade-off most users don't think about: your file lives on their server, even if briefly.

ConvertPivot takes a fundamentally different approach. Every conversion runs locally in your browser using WebAssembly — the same technology that gives desktop-class performance on the web. Your files never leave your device. There is no server, no cloud storage, no account, and no data collection.

This architecture is verifiable: open your browser's Developer Tools → Network tab during any conversion and confirm that zero file data is transmitted. Your sensitive bank statements, contact databases, and private notebooks stay private.

Complete Privacy

Because everything runs client-side, your files never cross a network boundary. Financial documents, medical records, and legal files stay on your machine — through every step, until you download the result. No third party ever sees your data.

No Limits, No Gates

There are no daily conversion caps, no file size limits, no watermarks, and no "upgrade to unlock" dialogs. The free tier handles files as large as your browser memory permits. Premium tiers exist only to support optional cloud restore features — never to degrade the free experience.

Works Everywhere

All 50+ tools work on desktop and mobile browsers. You can convert files on a Chromebook, a locked-down work laptop, an iPad, or an Android phone — no installation required.
