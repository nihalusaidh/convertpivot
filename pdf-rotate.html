How to Rotate PDF Pages

Drop a PDF file onto the rotator above. Each page appears as a preview card with its page number. Click the rotate button on any page to rotate it individually, or use the "Rotate All" buttons to apply the same rotation to every page at once.

When you are happy with the orientation, click "Download Rotated PDF". The pdf-lib library processes the PDF in your browser, applying the rotation metadata to each page. The result is downloaded automatically.

When to Rotate PDFs

Scanned documents often come in at odd angles. PDFs created from mobile phone photos may be sideways. Instead of printing and re-scanning, use this tool to fix page orientation in seconds. All common rotation angles are supported: 90 degrees clockwise, 90 degrees counterclockwise, and 180 degrees (upside down).
