Why Convert Images Online?

Whether you need to convert HEIC photos from your iPhone to universal JPG, or optimize WebP images for your website, browser-based conversion tools handle everything instantly. Privacy is at the core of our image converter tools. Every conversion runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript — no images are uploaded to any server, stored, or shared. You get fast, secure conversions with the same quality as desktop software, without the cost or complexity.

Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats do you support?

We support HEIC, AVIF, WebP, JPG, PNG, and SVG formats. You can convert between any of these formats, compress images, generate QR codes, encode images to Base64, and convert between color models.

Can I convert in bulk?

Most of our image converters support batch processing. You can select multiple images at once and convert them all simultaneously. Each conversion runs entirely in your browser, so bulk jobs are processed locally without slowing down your device.

Is my image uploaded to a server?

No. All image conversion is performed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device. This means your images remain private and secure at all times, and conversions happen quickly without needing to upload large files.

What is the maximum file size?

Since all processing happens locally in your browser, the maximum file size depends on your device's available memory. Most modern browsers can handle images up to 100MB or more. For extremely large images, ensure you have sufficient free RAM available.
