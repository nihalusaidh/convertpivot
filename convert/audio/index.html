Frequently Asked Questions

Which audio formats do you support?

We support conversion between MP3, WAV, FLAC, and OGG Vorbis formats. This covers the most commonly used audio formats for music, podcasts, audio editing, and professional production.

Is the conversion high quality?

Yes. Our converters use high-quality audio processing libraries that preserve the original sample rate, bit depth, and channel configuration. For lossless formats like WAV and FLAC, the output is bit-perfect.

Can I convert large audio files?

Yes, since all processing happens entirely in your browser, the maximum file size depends only on your device's available memory. Most modern computers can handle files of several hundred megabytes without issue.

No account or signup is required. All audio converters are completely free to use with no file uploads, no registration, and no usage limits. Your files never leave your device.
