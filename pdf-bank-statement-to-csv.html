How to Extract Transactions from Bank Statement PDFs

Upload your bank statement PDF (downloaded from your online banking portal). The converter extracts text from every page and parses transactions using pattern matching for dates, descriptions, and amounts. The result is a clean CSV ready for Excel, Google Sheets, or import into accounting software.

Note: This tool works best with text-based PDFs (the standard format from online banking). Scanned image PDFs may produce less reliable results. For the most accurate data, download OFX or QFX files directly from your bank when available.
