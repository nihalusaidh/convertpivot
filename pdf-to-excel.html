How to Convert PDF to Excel

Drop a PDF file onto the converter above. The tool extracts text from each page using PDF.js, then analyzes the text layout to identify table structures. Rows and columns are detected based on text position and spacing.

Preview the extracted data before downloading. You can choose between XLSX (Excel format with sheets) and CSV (universal plain-text format). The preview shows how your data will appear in the spreadsheet, letting you verify accuracy before committing to the download.

Tips for Best Results

Table extraction works best on text-based PDFs with clear column structures. Scanned documents (image-only PDFs) cannot be processed — they require OCR first. Financial statements, invoices, and data reports with regular table layouts produce the most accurate results. After conversion, you may need to adjust column widths and formatting in Excel.
