Working with Contact Files

VCF (vCard) is the universal standard for electronic business cards and contact information. Almost every email client, phone, and CRM supports vCard import and export. CSV is the most portable format for spreadsheet applications and database imports.

Converting between these formats is essential when migrating contacts between platforms — for example, exporting contacts from Gmail (VCF) to import into Excel for cleaning, or preparing a contact list from a CRM (CSV) to import into your phone (VCF).

Calendar Event Conversion

ICS (iCalendar) is the standard format for calendar events. Our ICS to CSV converter extracts event details like title, date, time, and description into rows for spreadsheet analysis. CSV to ICS lets you create calendar files from spreadsheet data for import into Google Calendar, Outlook, or Apple Calendar.
