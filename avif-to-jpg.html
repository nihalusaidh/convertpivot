How to Convert AVIF to JPG

AVIF (AV1 Image File Format) is the latest generation image format that offers up to 50% better compression than WebP and 70% better than JPEG. Despite its advantages, browser and application support remains inconsistent, especially on older devices.

Our converter renders the AVIF image at full resolution using the browser's native AVIF decoder and exports it as a high-quality JPEG. The process preserves original dimensions and color information while producing a universally compatible file.

This is particularly useful when you receive AVIF images from modern cameras or websites and need to share them with others who may not have AVIF-capable software.
