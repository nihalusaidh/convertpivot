Frequently Asked Questions

The average adult reads at about 200-250 words per minute (WPM) for comprehension. Skilled readers can reach 400-500 WPM. This calculator uses 238 WPM as the default, which is the average reading speed for English text.
Reading time is calculated by dividing the total word count by the reading speed in words per minute (WPM). For example, a 500-word article at 238 WPM takes approximately 2 minutes and 6 seconds to read.
Reading time helps readers decide if they have time to consume your content. Bloggers and content creators use estimated reading time to set expectations, improve user experience, and increase engagement. Most readers prefer articles under 7 minutes.
