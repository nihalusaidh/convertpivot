How to Convert QBO to CSV

Drop your QuickBooks QBO file onto the converter. All transactions are extracted with their full details: date, payee/description, amount, type (debit/credit), memo, and check number. The resulting CSV is ready to open in Excel, import into Google Sheets, or use with any accounting platform.

QuickBooks Web Connect files are downloaded directly from your bank's website through QuickBooks. They contain the same OFX-format transaction data as QFX files but packaged for QuickBooks import. Our converter strips away the QuickBooks-specific wrapper and extracts the raw transaction data.
