How to Convert Bank of America Statement to CSV

1

Download from BofA

Sign in to Bank of America online banking. Go to your account, click Statements & Documents, choose the statement period, and download the PDF version.

2

Drop into Converter

Drag your BofA statement PDF onto the drop zone or click to select a file. The converter reads the text and identifies each transaction row on every page.

3

Download CSV

Click Extract & Convert to CSV. All transactions are compiled into a CSV file ready for Excel, Google Sheets, or import into accounting tools like QuickBooks.

Tailored for Bank of America Statement Format

Bank of America statements use a clean, structured table layout that this converter is specifically designed to parse. It recognizes BofA's standard columns: transaction date, posting date, description, and amount. The converter also handles the summary sections at the top of each statement, extracting account number and statement period information when available. BofA credit card statements often include additional details like reference numbers and category codes — these are preserved in the output. Business account statements from Bank of America may include multiple sub-accounts or department-level transaction groupings, all of which are parsed page by page. For the best results, use the PDF downloaded directly from Bank of America's online banking portal. Statements that were printed and then scanned may not have a selectable text layer, which is required for the extraction process. The converter supports statement PDFs of any size and length, from single-page summaries to multi-year account histories.
