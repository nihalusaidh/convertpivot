How to Convert HEIC to JPG

Drop one or more HEIC photos from your iPhone, iPad, or Mac onto the converter. Each photo is decoded using the heic2any library and converted to high-quality JPEG format. Download individual images or convert them all at once.

HEIC is Apple's modern image format that offers ~50% better compression than JPEG. However, many websites, email clients, and Windows applications don't support it. Converting to JPG ensures your photos work everywhere — from uploading to websites to attaching in emails.

Batch Conversion Tips

Select multiple HEIC files at once by holding Ctrl (Cmd on Mac) while selecting files. Each image is converted independently and downloaded with the .jpg extension.
