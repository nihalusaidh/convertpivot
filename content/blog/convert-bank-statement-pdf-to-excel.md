Bank statements arrive as PDFs — that's just how banks work. But for accounting, budgeting, tax preparation, or financial analysis, you need those numbers in a spreadsheet. Manually typing dozens or hundreds of transactions is tedious and error-prone. This guide covers three ways to convert bank statement PDFs to Excel or CSV, from a free browser-based converter to using your bank's own data export tools.

**Can I convert PDF bank statement to Excel?** Yes. Use a dedicated PDF bank statement converter that extracts transaction data from the PDF's tabular layout and outputs CSV or Excel. The [ConvertPivot converter](https://convertpivot.com/pdf-bank-statement-to-csv) does this entirely in your browser.

**Is it safe to upload bank statements online?** Only if the converter processes files locally in your browser. Avoid uploading sensitive financial documents to unknown servers. ConvertPivot's tools use PDF.js to process everything client-side — your bank statements never leave your device.

**What is OFX format?** OFX (Open Financial Exchange) is a standardized XML-based format for financial transactions. Banks use it to export transaction data that can be imported into Quicken, GnuCash, and other accounting software. See our [OFX to CSV converter](https://convertpivot.com/ofx-to-csv) and [bank statement format guide](https://convertpivot.com/guides/bank-statement-formats) for more details.

**How to automate bank statement conversion?** Use your bank's direct export feature (OFX, QFX, QBO) instead of PDF for the most automated pipeline. Schedule monthly downloads and convert them in one batch. For PDFs that arrive automatically via email, use a consistent workflow with a browser-based converter that remembers your bank's layout.

**What is the most accurate way to convert PDF bank statements?** The most accurate method is your bank's own OFX/QFX export since the data comes directly from their system. For PDF-only banks, a purpose-built PDF bank statement converter is the next best option, far surpassing manual entry in both speed and accuracy.
